History of the Accident of Los Andes

On October 13, 1972, a Uruguayan plane carrying a rugby team and their families crashed in the Andes. The survivors fought for their survival in extremely adverse conditions, including freezing temperatures and food shortages.

After 72 days, they were finally rescued, having survived thanks to their determination, teamwork and, in some cases, making difficult decisions such as resorting to cannibalism to survive.
